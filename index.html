How To Play:

Goal
• Catch the Great yellow Star.
• Reach the target score.

Be Careful
• Avoid pink stars; they reduce 2 points.
• Time is limited, so move wisely.
• you must complete both of the goals to win.

Types of Star
• The Blue stars: Good stars that gives +1 points.
• The Green stars: Good stars that gives +2 points.
• The Pink stars: Bad stars that takes away -2 points.
• The Great star: The special star that ends the game.

Tips
• Every star matters. Choose your path carefully.
